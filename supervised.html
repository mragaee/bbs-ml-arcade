What's the ML idea here?

Supervised learning means learning from examples that come with the correct answers (labels). First you'll see training data — emails already stamped SPAM or HAM (not-spam). Your brain will pick up the patterns (ALL CAPS, "FREE!!!", sketchy senders…). Then you'll classify new, unlabeled emails — exactly what a trained spam filter does millions of times a day.
